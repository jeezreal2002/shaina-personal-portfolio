📸 HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Get Your Photos Ready
   - From your OJT report PDF
   - From your phone/camera
   - From Discovery Samal events
   - Total needed: 6 photos

STEP 2: Extract Photos from PDF (if needed)
   Method A: Screenshot Tool
      1. Open your OJT report PDF
      2. Press Windows + Shift + S
      3. Select and capture the photo
      4. Save as JPG

   Method B: PDF to Image Converter
      1. Visit: smallpdf.com/pdf-to-jpg
      2. Upload OJT report PDF
      3. Download all images
      4. Select best 6 photos

STEP 3: Rename Your Photos
   Rename them EXACTLY as shown (case-sensitive):
   
   1. banquet-setup.jpg
      (Photo of you during banquet service/setup)
   
   2. wedding-coordination.jpg
      (Photo from wedding events)
   
   3. haribar-service.jpg
      (Photo working at Haribar outlet)
   
   4. corporate-events.jpg
      (Photo from corporate functions)
   
   5. training-certification.jpg
      (Photo of training/certificates)
   
   6. team-collaboration.jpg
      (Photo with your team at Discovery Samal)

STEP 4: Add Photos to Folder
   1. Navigate to: assets\projects\
   2. Copy your 6 renamed photos
   3. Paste them into the projects folder
   4. Done!

STEP 5: View Your Portfolio
   1. Open index.html in browser
   2. Scroll to "Projects" section
   3. Your photos should appear!
   4. If not, press Ctrl + F5 to refresh
